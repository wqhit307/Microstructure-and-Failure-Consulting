Mechanical Testing

High-level interpretation of microstructure data using TEM, SEM, and other advanced techniques to understand material behavior and support informed engineering decisions.

Key Capabilities

  • TEM Analysis: SAED indexing, dislocation/twin/defect identification, precipitate/phase analysis, EDS/EELS, microstructure-property correlation
  • SEM/EBSD: Fractography, grain structure, phase distribution, texture/orientation
  • Metallography: Evaluation, heat-treatment assessment, inclusion/defect ID
