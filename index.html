Case Studies

Example: Fatigue Failure in Aerospace Component

A client experienced premature cracking in a high-cycle fatigue environment. We conducted SEM fractography, TEM dislocation analysis, and EBSD texture mapping.

Findings: Initiation at surface inclusions + striation spacing indicated overload during service. Microstructure showed non-uniform precipitation leading to localized weakness.

Recommendations: Adjust heat treatment for finer precipitates + implement inclusion control in upstream processing. Result: >3x fatigue life improvement in validation testing.
