Who We Are

Microstructure & Failure Consulting is led by Qiang, a materials engineer with extensive hands-on experience in advanced microstructure characterization using TEM and SEM, gained from working in a high-stakes nuclear laboratory environment in Belleville, Ontario.

With years of expertise in interpreting complex TEM/SEM data, identifying defects, phase transformations, and microstructure-property relationships, we bridge the gap between raw analytical results and practical engineering solutions. Our focus is on delivering clear, actionable recommendations that help clients optimize materials performance, prevent failures, and improve reliability in demanding applications.

We work with a small, trusted network of collaborators and accredited testing partners to ensure fast turnaround, strict confidentiality, and end-to-end project management — all while maintaining the highest standards of technical rigor and integrity.

Whether you're facing a challenging failure analysis, need expert guidance on mechanical testing, or want to outsource complex characterization workflows, we're here to turn your materials data into confident engineering decisions.
